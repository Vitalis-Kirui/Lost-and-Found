LOST ITEMS

Procedure of reporting lost items

When it comes to reporting a lost item,the following should be provided:

  • a clear and recent photo of the item
  • the exact name as it appears on the birth certificate in cases of missing children
  • serial number of item(s) lost if any
  • county or region of the lost occurence
  • date and time when last seen
  • any other unique features necessary
  • contact persons and any other helpful iformation that may not be listed
