LOST ITEMS

Procedure of reporting lost items

When it comes to reporting a lost item,the following should be provided:

  1. a clear and recent photo of the item
  2. the exact name as it appears on the birth certificate in cases of missing children
  3. serial number of item(s) lost if any
  4. county or region of the lost occurence
  5. date and time when last seen
  6. any other unique features necessary
  7. contact persons and any other helpful iformation that may not be listed
